Pacific EMIS Privacy Policy

Website

The only data collected by visits to the website are standard web analytics (e.g. Number of visits, location of visits, IP address of visits, pages on website visited, etc.) And this is collected on a private server, anonymous and not shared with anyone whatsoever.

Pacific Open Education Data app

Pretty simple. We do not collect anything when installing this app on the phone/tablet.
